Project Overview

This WebGIS provides an interactive visualization of demographic and business data for the Province of Cuneo, Piedmont, Italy.

  • 247 municipalities of the Province of Cuneo
  • Demographic data by age group
  • Business data by sector (Agriculture, Manufacturing, Transport)
  • Road and railway infrastructure

Data Source

  • ISTAT - Municipal demographic data 2022/2023
  • Business Registry - Active business locations 2025
  • OpenStreetMap - Base map
  • Regione Piemonte - Administrative boundaries

About Us

This project was developed to provide a territorial analysis tool for the Province of Cuneo.

Built with QGIS, Leaflet.js and Chart.js.

Help

  • Click on a municipality to see data in the right panel
  • Use the tabs to navigate between demographic and business data
  • Use IT/EN buttons to switch language
  • Use the filter to filter infrastructure
